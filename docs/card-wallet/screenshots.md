## Supported Card Types

OSS Card Wallet works with virtually any card:

- 💳 **Loyalty Cards**: Store rewards, grocery, retail
- 🎫 **Tickets**: Events, movies, concerts
- 🏥 **Insurance Cards**: Health, dental, vision
- 📚 **Library Cards**: Public libraries, memberships
- 🎁 **Gift Cards**: Restaurant, retail, online
- 🏢 **Membership Cards**: Gym, clubs, associations
- 🎟️ **Passes**: Transit, parking, access cards
- And many more!
