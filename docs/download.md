## System Requirements

### Android
- **Minimum**: Android 7.0 (API 24)
- **Recommended**: Android 10 or higher
- **Permissions**: Camera, Storage
- **Optional**: Biometric hardware for fingerprint/face unlock

### iOS
- **Minimum**: iOS 13.0
- **Recommended**: iOS 15 or higher
- **Compatible with**: iPhone, iPad
